ShadowBox — Reinforcement Learning for Sokoban

2026 · Individual Project

Problem: Train a stable RL agent for Sokoban with limited training time and difficult board states.

Outcome / Impact: Achieved strong performance gains on benchmark sets with better training efficiency.
