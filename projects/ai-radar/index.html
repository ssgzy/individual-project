AI Radar — Local AI Research Intelligence System

2026 – Ongoing · Individual Project

Problem: High-value AI updates are scattered across papers, repositories, feeds, and news sources.

Data: arXiv, GitHub, RSS, Hacker News, news feeds. Content is stored in raw and processed layers and scored by relevance.

Approach: Python local pipeline with ingestion, merge-dedup, automatic tagging, scoring, summarization flow, and scheduled report generation.

Outcome / Impact: Produced a stable workflow that supports reliable daily and weekly intelligence reports plus replaying failed items.

Your contribution: Designed architecture, implemented automation pipeline, structured output format, and prepared future extension points.
