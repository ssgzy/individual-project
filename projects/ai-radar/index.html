AI Radar — Local AI Research Intelligence System

2026 – Ongoing · Individual Project

Problem: AI updates were too fragmented across arXiv, GitHub, RSS, and news channels, making it hard to catch high-value signals quickly.

Data: Multi-source feeds from research papers, repositories, RSS, Hacker News, and finance/AI news. Data is staged in raw, cleaned, scored, and summarized layers.

Approach: Built a local Python pipeline with ingestion automation, deduplication, source scoring, summarization workflow, and scheduled reporting.

Outcome / Impact: Delivered daily and weekly outputs that are easier to review, prioritize, and act upon.

Your contribution: Designed the architecture, implemented the pipeline, organized metadata schemas, and prepared operational run logic for repeatable execution.
